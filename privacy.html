Privacy Policy

At ToolGram, your privacy is our fundamental principle. We've architected our entire platform to operate 100% client-side, meaning your data never leaves your browser. No data transmission to external servers occurs during any tool operation.

We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage patterns, or tool inputs. Your JSON data, passwords, code snippets, and files remain exclusively on your device throughout processing. This client-side architecture eliminates traditional privacy concerns associated with cloud-based services.

Since no data collection occurs, we have no databases storing user information, no analytics tracking, and no cookies for behavioral profiling. The only exception might be standard web server logs (like IP addresses) maintained by hosting providers, which are industry-standard and not under our direct control.

We believe privacy is a fundamental right, not a premium feature. That's why every ToolGram tool is designed from the ground up to respect your privacy while delivering professional-grade functionality.
